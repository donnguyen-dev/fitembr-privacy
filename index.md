# Fitembr Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 28 June 2026**

Fitembr ("we", "us", the "App") is a subscription fitness-coaching app that
provides AI-generated workout and wellness guidance. This policy explains what
information the App collects, how it is used, and who it is shared with. We have
tried to keep it short and accurate.

If you have any questions, contact us at **don@unswrc.com**.

## What we collect

You provide this information so the App can build and adjust your plan:

- **Profile details** — such as your name or nickname, age, sex, and fitness
  goals.
- **Body metrics** — height and weight (and changes you log over time).
- **Workout logs** — exercises, sets, reps, durations, and how sessions went.
- **Meal logs** — meals and foods you record.

We do **not** collect your data for advertising, and the App contains **no
ads** and no third-party advertising trackers.

> Fitembr v1 does **not** include any photo or camera-based calorie estimation
> feature. If such a feature is added later, this policy will be updated before
> it ships.

## How your data is stored

Your profile, body metrics, and workout/meal logs are stored **on your device**
using Apple's SwiftData. This data stays on your device except when it is sent
to our AI provider to generate guidance (see below), or when you have enabled
Apple services such as iCloud backup, which are governed by Apple's own privacy
policy.

## Sharing with our AI provider

To generate workout plans, suggestions, and coaching messages, the App sends
the relevant information you have provided — such as your profile, body
metrics, and workout/meal logs — to a **third-party AI service**. We currently
use:

- **OpenRouter** (https://openrouter.ai) as the API gateway, and
- **NVIDIA** AI models (Nemotron) accessed through that gateway.

This data is transmitted over an encrypted connection and is used to produce
your guidance. We do not control these providers' independent practices; please
review their privacy policies for details on how they process requests. We do
not sell your personal data, and we do not share it with these providers for
advertising.

AI output is automatically generated and may be inaccurate. It is general
fitness information, **not medical advice** — see the in-app Disclaimers.

## Subscriptions and payments

Fitembr is offered as an auto-renewable subscription. All billing is handled by
**Apple** through your App Store account. We never see or store your payment
card details. Purchase and subscription management is governed by Apple's terms
and privacy policy.

## Your choices and rights

- You can edit or delete the profile, metrics, and logs you have entered within
  the App.
- Deleting the App removes the data stored on your device. Data already sent to
  the AI provider to fulfil a request is subject to that provider's retention
  practices.
- To request help with your data, or to ask what we hold, email
  **don@unswrc.com**.

## Children

Fitembr is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in
your country) and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the App evolves. Material changes will be reflected
here with a new "Last updated" date.
